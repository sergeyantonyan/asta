Recycling makes us feel good, but few know what actually happens to a plastic bottle after we drop it into a bin We’re here to reduce the damage of disposal and ensure that waste is properly recycled… with full transparency

Why does this matter?

Typically, 40% of what’s put in the recycling bin is never recycled. It’s sorted and thrown out, partly due to contamination – a common issue that occurs when recyclables are mixed together in the same bin!

Where do we come in?

We built our own recycling process to make sure your recyclables change hands directly from our carriers to manufacturers who effectively reuse and recycle your items.

So what are you waiting for?

We recognize your efforts to save our planet so we want to ensure that your efforts translate into transparent and trackable results. See for yourself.

Recycling reinvented.

One of the most daunting issues humanity faces is a mounting waste problem, which not only pollutes the environment but also obstructs public health and threatens to drown poorer nations in unimaginable and irreparable toxicity. At Asta Works, we strive to reduce the damage of disposal. Reduce and reuse is always best but if that’s not an option, recycling is the move. Unfortunately, 60% of what’s put in the recycling bin ends up in the landfill. Yep, not great. That’s why we created a new process that reinvents recycling as you know it; by accepting pre-separated waste, we guarantee that more gets recycled, thus keeping the earth healthy and happy. You’re welcome. Not sold yet? We’ve simplified recycling by offering free pick-ups straight to your door, at the tap of your phone. Your recyclables amount to redeemable points with participating brands and our manufacturing partners utilize the materials to produce upcycled goods. Waste is effectively reused and our air, earth and oceans revert to their unspoiled beauty one plastic bag at a time. Let’s keep it natural (as it should be.) Our mission is to save the planet and reward you in the process.

A better process

Currently, of all the waste that is consumed, only 30% gets put into the recycling bin. From that, 60% is sent to the landfill. We built a better process that accepts separated materials to certify that your recyclables actually get recycled, allowing us to provide more transparency and data, effectively encouraging more recycling.

You already know this but just in case…

The environmental change sweeping the planet is occurring at a faster rate than previously predicted, making it imperative that we act now to reverse the otherwise unimaginable damage. Yes, the statistics are shocking. Recycling is an easy and effective way to help us combat ecological deterioration.

13,000 landfills

U.S. air and water supply is regularly polluted by 3,000 active and 10,000 idle landfills

1+ to 1 ratio

By 2050 there will be more plastic than fish in the world’s oceans

124 coal-fired power plants

U.S. landfills annually emit the equivalent in greenhouse gases as 124 coal-fired power plants

30% more resources

Humans consume 30% more resources today than what the planet can sustain
