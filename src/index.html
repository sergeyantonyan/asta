Recycling makes us feel good, but few know what actually happens to a plastic bottle after we drop it into a bin We’re here to reduce the damage of disposal and ensure that waste is properly recycled… with full transparency

Why does this matter?

Typically, 40% of what’s put in the recycling bin is never recycled. It’s sorted and thrown out, partly due to contamination – a common issue that occurs when recyclables are mixed together in the same bin!

Where do we come in?

We built our own recycling process to make sure your recyclables change hands directly from our carriers to manufacturers who effectively reuse and recycle your items.

So what are you waiting for?

Recycling reinvented

One of the most daunting issues humanity faces is a mounting waste problem, which not only pollutes the environment but also obstructs public health and threatens to drown poorer nations in unimaginable and irreparable toxicity. At Asta Works, we strive to reduce the damage of disposal. Reduce and reuse is always best but if that’s not an option, recycling is the move. Unfortunately, 60% of what’s put in the recycling bin ends up in the landfill. Yep, not great. That’s why we created a new process that reinvents recycling as you know it; by accepting pre-separated waste, we guarantee that more gets recycled, thus keeping the earth healthy and happy. You’re welcome. Not sold yet? We’ve simplified recycling by offering free pick-ups straight to your door, at the tap of your phone. Your recyclables amount to redeemable points with participating brands and our manufacturing partners utilize the materials to produce upcycled goods. Waste is effectively reused and our air, earth and oceans revert to their unspoiled beauty one plastic bag at a time. Let’s keep it natural (as it should be.) Our mission is to save the planet and reward you in the process. Think outside the box…. and recycle it.
